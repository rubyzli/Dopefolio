Project Overview

Undertaken as our inaugural freelance endeavor, the website's conception emerged through a collaborative partnership between myself and a fellow coding enthusiast from our intensive course. Hired by Spectrum Fenster Gmbh, our dedicated efforts spanned from January 2023 to March 2023. Employing React.js and Bootstrap, the frontend design exudes both sophistication and user-friendliness. Complementing this, the backend functionality was adeptly constructed using Express.js and Nodemailer, ensuring seamless communication.

Our operational methodology was grounded in the adoption of SCRUM principles, which fundamentally transformed our workflow.
